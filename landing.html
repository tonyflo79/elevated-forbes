Common Questions

Questions? We've Got Answers.

Everything you need to know about Forbes Wireless.

How does eSIM activation work?

After signup, you'll receive an eSIM activation link via email. Simply scan the QR code or tap the link on your iPhone, and your service activates instantly. No physical SIM required. No store visit. Your existing number can be transferred seamlessly—our concierge team handles the port request for you.

What network does Forbes Wireless use?

Forbes Wireless is powered by Verizon's network—America's most reliable network for coverage, speed, and 5G. You get the same premium network performance as Verizon's direct customers, with unlimited talk, text, and 5G data. No throttling, no compromises.

What devices are compatible?

Forbes Wireless works with any eSIM-compatible iPhone (iPhone XS and later) or Android device. This includes all recent flagship devices from Apple, Samsung, Google Pixel, and more. If you're unsure about your device's compatibility, our concierge team can verify before you sign up.

What is the NYC Concierge and how does it work?

The Concierge is your AI-powered NYC intelligence. Ask anything about the city—best coffee in FiDi, where locals eat in Midtown, hidden speakeasies, Sunday plans in Brooklyn. Get instant, insider recommendations from an assistant that knows NYC like a lifelong New Yorker. It's like having a local friend who's been everywhere and knows everyone.

How are the perks curated?

Our perks team negotiates exclusive member benefits with premium partners across dining, travel, wellness, entertainment, and professional services. These aren't generic discounts—they're VIP-level access, priority reservations, and meaningful savings at venues and services that matter to professionals. New perks are added regularly based on member feedback.

What's the difference between Professional and Business tiers?

Professional ($60/month) includes unlimited Verizon network access, $200+/month in curated perks across dining, fitness, travel, food, and events, plus NYC Concierge AI and full app access. Business ($80/month) adds priority concierge support, exclusive Forbes networking events, and enhanced venue booking capabilities.

Can I keep my current phone number?

Absolutely. We handle the number porting process for you. During activation, provide your current carrier details and we'll coordinate the transfer. Your number typically moves within 24-48 hours with zero interruption to service.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No long-term contracts. Forbes Wireless is month-to-month billing. You can cancel anytime without early termination fees. We believe in earning your business every month, not locking you into commitments.

What kind of support is available?

All members receive access to our concierge support team via in-app chat and email. Business tier members get priority response and phone support. We also provide comprehensive self-service resources in the app for account management, billing, and troubleshooting.

How do I get started?

Click 'Apply for Membership' and complete the brief application. Once approved, you'll receive your digital welcome kit with eSIM activation instructions. The entire process—from application to exploring NYC with your personal Concierge—can happen in under 24 hours.
